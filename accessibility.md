# Accessibility Statement

_Last updated: September 27, 2025_

The Green Olive Bar and Grill is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

---

## Measures to support accessibility

- We strive to ensure our website is usable by people of all abilities.
- We regularly review our website to improve accessibility.
- We provide alternative text for all images and non-text content.
- We aim to maintain appropriate color contrast and readable font sizes.

---

## Conformance status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. The Green Olive Bar and Grill website aims to conform to WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards.

## Limitations and alternatives

Despite our best efforts to ensure accessibility of The Green Olive Bar and Grill website, there may be some limitations. Please contact us if you observe an issue so we can address it promptly.

---

## Assessment approach

The Green Olive Bar and Grill assesses the accessibility of this website through self-evaluation and periodic review.

---

## Commitment

We are committed to making our website accessible to all users and will continue to improve our accessibility features.
